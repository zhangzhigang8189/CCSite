HSBC Arch Career Framework

HSBC Arch Career Framework

Research direction

  • Enterprise Architects
    create target state architectures and roadmaps aligned with business and technology strategy Enterprise Architects integrate expertise across the full range of more specific architecture disciplines
  • Business Architects
    model target state organisation of business capabilities, services, functions, processes and information
  • Data Architects
    perform data modelling, comprising data definition, structure, flow and semantics
  • Solution Architects
    own the architectural design of end-to-end IT solutions to meet business requirements
  • Security Architects
    create security patterns and security solution architectures aligned to Group standards
  • Technical (Infrastructure, Domain or Application) Architects
    provide architectural direction and solutions to technical platforms, services, patterns and key application components

Architect level

  1. Associate Architect

    Indicative GCB Band range:GCB 4-6

    GLT Equivalent: No equivalent position in GLT

  2. Architect

    Indicative GCB Band range: GCB 4-5

    GLT Equivalent: GLT Consultant Specialist

  3. Lead Architect

    Indicative GCB Band range: GCB 4-5

    GLT Equivalent: GLT Senior Consultant Specialist

  4. Principal Architect

    Indicative GCB Band range: GCB 3-4

    GLT Equivalent: GLT Lead Consultant Specialist

  5. Head of Architecture

    Indicative GCB Band range: GCB 2-3

    GLT Equivalent: No equivalent position in GLT

Architect incubation plan

Architect incubation plan

Architect incubation plan（HighLevel）

  1. 每一个培训架构师分配一个HSBC的导师，建立导师指导制。
  2. HSBC导师对分配到的人员进行一次会谈，和被指导人一起确认后期的发展方向、所需的培训内容和相应的培养计划。
  3. HSBC和能力中心根据会谈的结果，制定整体的培训内容和培养计划。
